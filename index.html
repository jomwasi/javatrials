GHANIAN NAMING SYSTEM

Africa is a continent that derives it's strenth in socialising. There's always a reason to gather and make merry!
Naming is one of those valued cultural practices that families travel form far and wide to attend.
Every tribe has their own method of naming. Some name according to birth time while others name after parents or prominent people of the tribe.
Akan is an ethnic group in Ghana in West Africa. They name according to the day of the week.

Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
Most people do not know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born.
Luckily for us, there are ways to calculate the day of the week from a specific date.
