GHANIAN NAMING SYSTEM

Africa is a continent that derives it's strenth in socialising. There's always a reason to gather and make merry! Naming is one of those valued cultural practices that families travel form far and wide to attend. Every tribe has their own method of naming. Some name according to birth time while others name after parents or prominent people in the tribe. Akan is an ethnic group in Ghana in West Africa. They name according to the day of the week.
